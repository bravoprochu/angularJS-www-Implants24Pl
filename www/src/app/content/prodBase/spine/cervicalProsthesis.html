A monobloc total that restores

The ESP disc prosthesis is made of 2 titanium alloy end-plates and an elastomeric cushion. The spikes on the end-plates outer surfaces improve primary fixation. The combination of a hydroxyapatite (HA) coating on top of a T40 rough surface are considered as one of the best existing coating insuring good bony fixation over time. The titanium alloy used for the end plates allows clear medical imaging.

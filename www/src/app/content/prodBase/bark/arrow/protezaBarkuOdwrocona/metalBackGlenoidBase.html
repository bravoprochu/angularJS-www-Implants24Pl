DEFINITIVE IMPLANTS

Put the definitive metal-back glenoid base into place using the glenosphere handle (ref. 261 101), and then impact it using the impactor (refs. 261 009 and 261 081). Drill with the Ø 3.2 mm bit (ref.261 065).
The best bone fixation areas can be sought because of tolerance of 20°.

  • The upper Ø5.5mm cancellous screw aims for the base of the coracoid.
  • The lower Ø5.5mm cancellous screw aims for the pillar of the scapula.

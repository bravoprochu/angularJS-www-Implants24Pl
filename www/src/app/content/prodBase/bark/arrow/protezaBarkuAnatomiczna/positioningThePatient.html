The success of a shoulder prosthesis depends on its ability to reproduce normal articular bone anatomy and re-establish equilibrium between the soft tissues. With its wide range of sizes of humeral stems, centred and off-centred humeral heads and glenoid implants, the ARROW prosthesis can be adapted to any morphology.
Its well-designed shape and the preservation of bone mass means that the ARROW prosthesis is fixed stably, guaranteeing an optimal life span.

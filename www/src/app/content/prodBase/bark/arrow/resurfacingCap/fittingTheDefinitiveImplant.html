Fitting the definitive implant

After filling any loss of bone substance using the bone removed during reaming, place the definitive cap in position, carefully checking the correct orientation of the pins relative to bony marking.

Gradually impact it, checking that the cap does not tip at an angle.

Reduction.

The subscapularis is closed and a drain inserted if required.

EXTRACTION OF THE IMPLANT

Extraction is possible using the head forceps.

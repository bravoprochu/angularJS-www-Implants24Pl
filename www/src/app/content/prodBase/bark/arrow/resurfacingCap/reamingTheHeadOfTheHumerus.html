REAMING THE HEAD OF THE HUMERUS First REAMING: T+1

Reaming is guided by the pin. It is performed solely using the motor.

Begin using the reamer with a diameter next above the predefined size.

SUBSEQUENT REAMING FOR SIZE CORRECTION

Still guided by the pin, subsequent reaming must penetrate into the head of the humerus as far as the anatomical neck, thus leaving a 1 to 2 mm bony step around the entire circumference of the head of the humerus.

- Ream to the anatomical neck.
- If there is no circumferential margin, ream the next size down.
- Undertake final ablation of residual osteophytes.

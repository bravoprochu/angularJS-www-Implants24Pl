Product line

The PHARO bipolar head product line consists of mobile and bipolar cups that either have a fixed or adjustable neck.

PHARO mobile cups:

  • 10 sizes ranging from 41 to 59 mm by 2-mm increments
  • Are combined with a line of 28-mm stainless steel heads that has 6 different neck lengths, which provides the surgeon with a wide range of adjustments.

PHARO bipolar cups with adjustable neck:

  • 10 sizes ranging from 41 to 59 mm (2-mm increments). The sleeve provides 15 mm of adjustment.

PHARO bipolar cups with fixed neck:

  • 10 sizes ranging from 41 to 59 mm (2-mm increments).
Features and materials

PHARO mobile cups consist of a liner and a removable HDPE ring that are covered by stainless steel plating.

PHARO bipolar cups with adjustable neck are made of 316L stainless steel.

PHARO bipolar cups with fixed neck are made of 316L stainless steel.

Instrumentation

Each PHARO stem model has its own scaled down instrumentation set.

TRADITIONAL POLYETHYLENE INSERTS

The ATLAS metal-back range (III P, IVP or MS), combined with a wide range of traditional UHMWPE polyethylene inserts in sizes 40 to 74mm, with internal diameters of 22,22mm; 28mm; 32mm.

STABILITY

The stability of the insert is, obviously, essential. To avoid the insert creeping, it has been designed with a cylindrical peripheral zone and a rough interior surface on the metal-back, created using alumina blasting. These technical elements have proved their effectiveness over the past 20 years and over 150,000 implantations. This fixation ensures that there is no micro-movement of the insert within the metal-back, proven by observing the machining lines, which are still visible, on inserts removed after over 10 years.

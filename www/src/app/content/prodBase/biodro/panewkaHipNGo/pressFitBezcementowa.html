PRESS-FIT DUAL MOBILITY CEMENTLESS ACETABULAR CUP

The dual mobility concept was invented by Prof. Gilles Bousquet more than twenty years ago. It is based on the combination of two principles:
- Joint stability
- Reduction of wear
FH ORTHOPEDICS has reworked this concept, this time taking into account the technological progress made since the 1970s in terms of the materials used, so as to obtain improved tribological results.
The Hip’n go dual mobility acetabular cup comes in a large range and makes it possible to satisfy a maximum of indications.

CONCEPT

The universal Hip’n go concept makes it possible to choose, in either the pre-operative or per-operative period, between a dual mobility acetabular cup (press-fit or tripod) and a rigid acetabular cup (Biolox Delta® ceramic or UHMWPE insert).

RANGE and MATERIALS

The range of dual mobility cementless acetabular cups comes in 10 sizes, ranging from a diameter 46 mm to 64 mm in 2 mm increments. The inserts are available with an inner diameter of 22.2 mm for size 46 and an inner diameter of 28 mm for sizes 48 to 64 mm. • The metal-backs are made from mirror polished CoCr (cobalt chromium) on the inner face to provide mobilisation of the insert with maximum tribological properties.
The outside surface has a T40 titanium spray and a hydroxyapatite coating to increase both the implant’s secondary fixation capacities and its lifespan*.
• The inserts are made from UHMWPE (Ultra High Molecular Weight PolyEthylene).

PRESS-FIT

The metal-back of the Hip’n go dual mobility acetabular cup is a semicircular cup that is flared at the equator because of a serrated macrostructure that provides it with excellent press-fit capacities and perfect primary fixation. The press-fit is increased by three peripheral wings, which also prevent any possible rotation of the cup in the receiving bone.

* Detailed technical sheet available

STABILITY

In order to apply the stability principles developed by Prof. Bousquet, the semi-circular part of the cup is elongated in its upper part by a 4mm lip, which helps to improve the congruence of the system.

INSERT

The minimum thickness of the polyethylene inserts is 8 mm, to prevent any risk of creep. The geometry of the inserts has been studied to make maximum head/insert clearance possible, as well as linear contact insert/neck as a means of reducing wear. It is for this reason that the inserts are equipped with an inner bevelled edge. These inserts come in two diameters : 22.2 mm for size 46 and 28 mm for sizes 48 and up.

INSTRUMENTATION

The specific Hip’n go instruments set can be used with both types of cementless dual mobility acetabular cups proposed in this programme: press-fit and tripod.
This instrument set allows surgeons to use every approach available, including mini-invasive approaches.
Impaction tools are available, either for posterior or anterior approaches

FHK philosophy

The FHK total knee is a third generation prosthesis resolutely turned towards the user, based on the most widely validated principles of prosthetic knee surgery.

FHK range offers a choice of implants fixed bearing (PS and CR) and mobile bearing, to match to philosophies of surgeons and most frequent indications.

FHK Instrumentation do not impose anything, but is adapted to the most common technical choices respecting the principles necessary to the specifications of prosthetic surgery of the knee.

Fixed bearing

The system of postero-stabilisation allows reduce the risk of dislocation without excessive stress on the base. Femoral cam is located in an area to reduce the leverage on the polyethylene and thus loosening constraints. Postero-stabilisation mechanism allows to preserve bone stock.

Mobile bearing

Optimal congruency allows to reduce the risk of wear respecting knee biomechanics. The system for posterior stabilization by congruency, while providing anteroposterior stability, reduces polyethylene wear and to preserve the femur bone.

Patella

Patella implant is available in 2 versions:

  • resurfacing
  • inlay
It has been designed to fit to the prostetic trochlea groove decreasing the risk of anterior compartment pressure.

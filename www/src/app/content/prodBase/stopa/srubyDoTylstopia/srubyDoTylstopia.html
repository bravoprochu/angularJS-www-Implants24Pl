SELF-COMPRESSING SCREW FOR HINDFOOT OSTEOSYNTHESIS
  • The be POD range of self-compressing hindfoot screws was designed for the osteosynthesis of the hindfoot, providing an osteosynthesis result that is stable, reliable and easy to achieve.
  • This range can be used in a wide variety of indications :
    - Hindfoot osteosynthesis, fixation of mono and bicortical osteotomies,
    - Stabilisation of various types of fracture (e.g. : calcaneum, talus…),
    - Subtalar, or talocrural arthrodeses…
  • Designed in a titanium alloy (TA6V), these screws are fully biocompatible.
  • The anodisation of the screws helps ease of insertion and eventual removal of the implants.
  • The screws are delivered in sterile, individually wrapped packs allowing the strictest traceability standards.

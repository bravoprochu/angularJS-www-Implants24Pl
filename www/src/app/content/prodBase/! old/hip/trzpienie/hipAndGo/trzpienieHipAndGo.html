Cemented femoral stem

The cemented Thira® stem is made of stainless steel 316L ; the surface is highly polished as ratest cementing techniques require.

The shape is selflocking and has a tapered shape allowing for optimal anteroposteral filling. The cervico diaphyseal angle is 130°. It exists in 9 different sizes, from 2 to 10 (size 1 is available on special request). The necks are of increasing length for efficient adaptation to the patients’ anatomy.

The cemented Thira® stem has the same geometry as the cementless Thira® stem, the arthroplasty can therefore be performed with the same instrumentation, whether the stem is cemented or not.

  • Cervico diasphyseal angle of 130°.
  • 12/14 taper allowing an alumina/alumina couple to be used
  • Diameter of neck : 12.5, in order to avoid impingements
